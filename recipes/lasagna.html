Lasagna Recipie:

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook lasagna noodles
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish
  6. Spoon 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the sauce
  7. Spoon 1/3 of the marinara sauce over the ricotta mixture
  8. Sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce
  9. Repeat layers twice
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
